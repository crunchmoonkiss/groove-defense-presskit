About the Game

Build Your Defense. Compose Your Victory.

Groove Defense is a tower defense game where placing towers doesn't just protect your base, it creates the music. Each instrument tower you place adds a new layer to a dynamic, evolving soundtrack that builds as you play.

Choose from four distinct musical genres: 80s, 2010s, Slasher Flick, and Synthwave, each with unique towers, enemies, and sonic palettes. Place a drum tower and hear the beat drop. Add a bass tower to fill out the low end. Stack synths to create soaring melodies. Your defense strategy becomes your composition.

Enemies move to the beat, so timing matters. Every session creates a different track based on your choices, making each playthrough a unique musical experience.

Three modes keep the experience fresh: Tour Mode for a structured campaign, Free Play for open experimentation, and Rhythm Remix, a VSMR mode that strips away the defense and lets you create freely with the tools you've unlocked.

Key Features
  • Every tower is a real instrument. Placement builds a dynamic, layered soundtrack in real time
  • Four musical genres (80s, 2010s, Slasher Flick, Synthwave) each with distinct towers, enemies, and vibes
  • Three modes: Tour Mode (campaign), Free Play (sandbox), and Rhythm Remix (VSMR mode)
  • Enemies move on beat, so timing your placement matters
  • Towers fire in rhythm, creating both strategic and musical feedback loops
  • Every playthrough generates a unique track based on your tower choices

About the Developer

Crunch Moonkiss Studios is a one-person indie studio founded by an award-winning film composer with festival selections and a Best Composer win. Groove Defense combines a deep background in music composition with a passion for game design, born from the idea that players should be the ones building the score, not just listening to it.
